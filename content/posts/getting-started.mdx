# Getting Started with Creator Campaigns on Maakeit

Welcome to Maakeit! Whether you're a brand looking to collaborate with creators or a creator seeking exciting campaigns, this guide will help you get started.

## For Brands: Launching Your First Campaign

Launching a creator campaign on Maakeit is straightforward:

1. **Create your campaign brief** - Define your goals, deliverables, and budget
2. **Post to the marketplace** - Your campaign goes live for creators to discover
3. **Review applications** - Creators apply with their profiles and past work
4. **Select creators** - Choose the best fit for your brand
5. **Collaborate & approve** - Use built-in messaging and content approval
6. **Automatic payment** - Creators get paid instantly upon approval

## For Creators: Finding Your Next Campaign

As a creator on Maakeit, you have access to campaigns from top brands:

- Browse the campaign marketplace
- Filter by niche, budget, and content type
- Apply with one click using your creator profile
- Get selected by brands that love your work
- Deliver content and get paid instantly

## Why Maakeit is Different

**Fair Pricing**: Creators keep 100% of earnings. Brands pay a small 2% handling fee.

**Instant Payments**: Powered by Paystack for automated, instant settlements.

**Transparent Process**: Clear expectations, standardized contracts, built-in collaboration tools.
